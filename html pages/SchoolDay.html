  • Please Note

    1. Students who enter school are not allowed to leave the campus.
    2. Students who are not in proper uniform are not allowed to enter the school unless they have a note from their parents explaining why they are not in uniform.
    3. Students should be neat as they enter the school. Boys should have their shirts tucked in before they enter the School.


SCHOOL DAY:

7:30 AM - Arrival Time for students.

7:30 AM - 7:50 AM - Homeroom Session. All students in the class

7:50 AM - Students should line up from their class to assembly

7:55AM - The morning assembly begins. Tardy Penalties Start.

8:05AM - First lesson starts.

There are two break times in the day. After which, students must line up on the assembly to get ready for the next class. Students should only leave the assembly with their teacher.

Please Note:

    1. Afternoon picks up for all students will take place at the same entry gate as is used for morning entry.
    2. N- Grade 3 students will be handed over to a parent or adult who produces the parental consent card.
    3. Please note that no child will be able to change their afternoon pick up arrangements without the written notification and consent by that child’s parent to both the class teacher and the Section Principal. This especially applies to children who use the school bus.

    EARLY DISMISSAL PROCEDURE

    In the event of an emergency dismissal of students due to predicted adverse weather conditions or as required by the Ministry of Education, an SMS will be sent to all parents from the central administration.

    1. Elementary and Middle School: students are kept in the classroom until their drivers or parents arrive.
    2. In High School: students who have their own means of transports are allowed to leave the campus. The rest of the students remain in the classroom until their parents/drivers arrive.
